The Rio Scale of Extraterrestrial Contacts

IAA SETI Permanent Committee: Rio Scale (Iván Almár and Jill Tarter, in the 51st International Astronautical Congress, 29th Review Meeting on the Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence, in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, in October, 2000) is a scale for assessing putative contacts with extraterrestrial intelligent entities. It was devised in analogy with several other such scales, and the higher a putative contact scores, the greater is its importance.
