It has been difficult to find good self-diagnosis tools for autism and Asperger's syndrome, so I (LP) have taken some published papers and turned their questionnaires into HTML pages.

WARNING! These tests should not be construed as providing a medical or psychiatric diagnosis. They are here for educational and entertainment purposes.

In the test pages, click on the buttons for all the possibilities and then click on "Calculate your score!" It will appear below that button.

The tests:

  • AQ: Autism Quotient How close to autistic are you?
  • EQ: Empathy Quotient How sensitive to others' emotions are you?
  • FQ: Friendship and Relationship Quotient How concerned are you with these?
  • SQ: System Quotient How systematic are you?
  • SQ-R: Revised version of SQ With a broader range of questions

Note that the AQ, EQ, and SQ tests have a popup menu near the "Calculate" button for selecting the scoring type; either (Strongly) = (Slightly) or (Strongly) = 2*(Slightly). However, for each test, this popup menu is initially set to whatever I've been able to find numbers for.

Also, some American equivalents have been added for a few Anglicisms, like motorways (highways), D.I.Y. (Do It Yourself projects), and ministries (government agencies).

Both sexes have been grouped together in the Asperger's category, since their scores are rather similar.

However, they are separated in the Normal category, since their scores are often noticeably different:

  • m: male
  • f: female
